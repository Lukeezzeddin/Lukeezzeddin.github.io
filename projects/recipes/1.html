My Favorite Charcoal Recipe

I've selected this recipe because it's been in my family for a long time. There's been so many times where I come home from school and my mom has baked me some charcoal before I head off to football. As my mom always says, If you eat charcoal, you be barcoal

Tips, Tricks, and Substitutions

  • If you don't have Oak wood, Hickory or Maple also taste good
  • You want to make your charcoal in a dry, well ventilated room to make it burn more easily
Ingredients
10 lbs of wood
0.5 lbs of newspaper
fire starter

Recipe
place your wood in a tepee pattern
place the crumpled up newspaper inside the tepee
use the fire starter on the newspaper. this should start a fire
wait until the fire isn't hot anymore
enjoy!

This recipe makes 1 serving and takes about 2 hours
