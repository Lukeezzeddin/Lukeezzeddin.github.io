My Favorite Charcoal Wood Chips Recipe

I've selected this recipe because it's been in my family for a long time. There's been so many times where I come home from school and my mom has baked me some wood chips before I head off to football. As my mom always says, If you eat wood, you be good

Tips, Tricks, and Substitutions

  • If you don't have an Oak tree, Hickory or Maple also taste good
  • You want to make your wood in a wet, sunny room to let it grow better
Ingredients
1 sapling
Dirt
Water

Recipe
place your sapling in your dirt
water the sapling every day
wait until the sapling is 400lbs
chop the tree into wood chips
enjoy!

This recipe makes 1 serving and takes about 20+ years
